# Quick Debrief Mode

When the instructor says "quick debrief" or seems rushed, compress to 5 questions:

1. **What happened?** Stick to plan or deviate? (one sentence)
2. **What worked?** Best moment of the class. (one sentence)
3. **What flopped?** Worst moment or thing you'd change. (one sentence)
4. **SET: agenda, recap, availability?** (three yes/no answers)
5. **Anything due before next class?** (action items)

Produce the same four outputs but keep the class log to one paragraph per section.
